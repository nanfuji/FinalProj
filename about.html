About

Tablo Kitchen x Café

Tablo Kitchen x Cafe is a fairly new chain of restaurants with three branches in the south of the Metro and one in Quezon City. They offer modern Filipino comfort food and have a tropical-inspired interior, which is very Instagramable.

The Must Tries

  • Boneless Peri-Peri Chicken
  • Cream Cheese Wedges
  • Roast Beef Adobo Cream

Mesa Calle Bistro

The MESA threefold advantage can be summed up as innovative yet traditional cuisine, genuine Filipino hospitality, and an exceptional dining experience highlighted by native accents all within the realm of affordable luxury.

The Must Tries

  • Baby squid sautéed in olive oil
  • Kare Kare Beef and Tripe
  • Pansit Canton

Manam

Home of the best sisig in Manila, Manam specializes in serving up Filipino food in classics and twists, perfect for any occasion. A homegrown concept of the Moment Group, the comfort Filipino food restaurant is highly rated and recommended by top food and travel sites. Among its most popular offerings are its signature House Crispy Sisig and Sinigang na Beef Short Rib and Watermelon.

The Must Tries

  • House Crispy Sisig
  • Sinigang na Beef Short Rib and Watermelon
  • Crispy Pancit Palabok

Las Casas Quezon City

Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar Quezon City is the first venture of the Marivent Hotels and Resorts Inc. under the Las Casas Heritage Collection. It first opened its door to the public in February 2018. Las Casas Quezon City aligns with Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar in Bagac, Bataan's patriotic advocacy of preserving and restoring Spanish-Filipino Houses built in the 18th-century era.

The Must Tries

  • Lumpia Ni Nanay
  • Home Made Pizza
  • Fruity Cocktails
