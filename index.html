Top 5 Restaurants in Manila

Tablo Kitchen x Café

Tablo Kitchen x Café is a community cafe and restaurant. Designed and created to be a place of creativity, community, and comfort food. They offer a space for people to come together as it offers a very homey vibe. They even have a pet-friendly space in case you want to bring your beloved pets when you come together with your friends or family.

Scout Borromeo, Quezon City – 14a Scout Borromeo St, Diliman, Quezon City, Metro Manila

Mesa Calle Bistro

Mesa has been a household name especially when it comes to Filipino cuisines. Their elevated take on some of our favorites has become our comfort and pride. Now pair that off with an amazing interior, then you get the ultimate time of your life.

Ever Commonwealth Center Mall Calle Bistro, Commonwealth Avenue corner Don Antonio drive Batasan Hills, Quezon City

Manam

True to the Manam brand, Manam Café continues to serve Filipino favorites. But what sets this apart from the always packed restaurant is that it has a more casual vibe and more options for lighter meals.

Ground Floor, Podium Mall, ADB Avenue, Ortigas Center, Pasig City.

Las Casas Quezon City

Even if you haven’t visited Las Casas in Bataan, you’ll probably have seen many photos of it everywhere. But you don’t have to go far anymore because right smack in the metro is Las Casas Quezon City. This events place, museum, and restaurant has the same exact old-world charm that everyone has come to love about its Bataan origins.

134 Roosevelt Ave, San Francisco del Monte, Quezon City

Kanto Freestyle Breakfast

Are you one of those who simply loves breakfast dinners? Well, then Kanto Freestyle Breakfast is the perfect place for you. This is a popular spot that serves breakfast 24/7! What is so great about Kanto is that you can build your own dish from its vast selection of breakfast items. Meaning ‘street corner’ in English, Kanto is a local term for a spot where friends meet up and enjoy a delicious breakfast.

2466 Leon Guinto Street, Malate, Manila, Metro Manila
